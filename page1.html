Payroll and Compensation

This segment is only made visible to staff who are your employees.

Staff are categorized into 2; Employee and Vendor Contractor

Employee : Staff under your direct employment.
Vendor Contractor : Staff that receive compensation from a 3rd party, which your company is in contract with.

Your employees (under your company payroll) navigates to this link to view their pays/earnings. 4 links are minimally placed here;
Pay Slip, Tax, Compensation,
& Employee Handbook (Compensation & Benefits section)
